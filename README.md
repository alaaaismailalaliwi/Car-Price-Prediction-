A **car price prediction model** is a type of machine learning model designed to estimate the market value of a car based on its features and historical data.

It analyzes various factors such as the car’s brand, manufacturing year, mileage, engine size, fuel type, transmission, and overall condition. By learning patterns from previously sold cars, the model can predict the expected selling price of a new or used vehicle.

This type of model is commonly used in automotive marketplaces, dealerships, and online platforms to help users make informed buying or selling decisions, improve pricing accuracy, and reduce human estimation errors.
